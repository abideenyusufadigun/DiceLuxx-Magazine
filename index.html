A Brief History

Of the Store

  • V1 - 2021

    DiceLuxx was born from a simple vision: to create a dependable hub where technology and automotive innovation meet quality and efficiency. What started as a small gadget-focused idea gradually evolved into a brand driven by the need to provide premium tech and auto solutions for modern lifestyles.

  • V2 - 2022

    DiceLuxx positioned itself not just as a seller, but as a trusted supplier of high-performance gadgets.

  • V3 - 2023

    DiceLuxx was redefined from a deep fascination with movement, machines, and modern innovation — a journey that began with a simple passion for powerbike riding.

  • V4 - 2024

    We created a brand that merges technology and automotive excellence. DiceLuxx emerged as a response to the need for reliable, premium gadgets and auto accessories that complement modern living while celebrating the legacy of innovation that began with early motorized transport.

  • V5 - 2025

    DiceLuxx entered a new phase of growth and refinement. The brand expanded its offerings, introducing more advanced tech gadgets, smarter auto accessories, and high-performance equipment tailored for both everyday users and enthusiasts. A stronger focus was placed on efficiency, with improved store organization, faster service processes, and a more customer-centered shopping experience.

  • V6 - 2026

    In 2026, DiceLuxx officially launched as a full-scale Tech & Auto Lifestyle Store, bringing its vision to life. This launch represented the culmination of passion, preparation, and innovation — a physical and digital space where technology meets motion, and efficiency meets excellence.
